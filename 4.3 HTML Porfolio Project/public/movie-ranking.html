The Best Movies According to Lars

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lord of the Rings - The fellowship of the ring

In my opinion the best in the Lord of the Rings triology and the best fantasy movie off all time
I love the combination of practical effects and early CGI

Star Wars - Empire Strikes Back

The best of all of the Star Wars movies. An all time classic, and it still holds up to the standards of the times.

Rock'n Rolla

Guy Richie at his best. I love the dialog and the humor.
